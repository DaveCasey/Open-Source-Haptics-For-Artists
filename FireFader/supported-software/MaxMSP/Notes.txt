*** Make sure that Fader~.maxpat is opening the correct serial interface! ***

For more examples, please download HSPv2 (you will need it anyway in order to use these models.)

In Max/MSP 5, remember to set the signal vector size to 1 and the Scheduler Interval and Slop (see Scheduler settings under preferences) to 1ms.

You will need to turn DSP on.
